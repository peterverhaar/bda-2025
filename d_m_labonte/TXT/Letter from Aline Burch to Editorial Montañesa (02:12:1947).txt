MS 2750/217/3/80




AB                     2nd December, 1947

Editorial Montanesa
Fernandez de la Hoz 4, 
Madrid, Spain. 

Dear Sirs, 

Thank you for your letter of 
24 November enquiring for the
Spanish rights in Mr. Kitchin's
books. We only hold the rights 
in one of these 'Death of My Aunt' -
and the Spanish rights in this title 
are already sold. For the rights 
in his other books, you should apply
to Mr. Kitchin's agents, Messrs.Pearn
Pollinger & Higham, Ltd., 39/40
Bedford Street, Strand, London W.C.2.

                    Yours faithfully,
                    
           
                    for the Hogarth Press Ltd.
