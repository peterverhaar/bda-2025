About Me

Hello! I'm Yiling Chen (陈依灵 in Chinese) and come from China. I studied Chinese Language and Literature for my BA in Nanjing University, which is located at Nanjing, a beautiful city with many museums and well-preserved old Chinese architecture. Besides, I've spent one semester in The University of Melbourne as an exchange student, where I did some courses in creative writing and film studies.

In my free time, I love reading poetry, writing, watching films, collecting old photos and visiting museums. I enjoy wandering around the cities or countryside to look for something interesting. Meanwhile I always like to travel and explore different culture and society. I've travelled to England, Australia, Indonesia and many other wonderful countries. Actually I have already visited the Netherlands and other European countries in my 14 and now I'm doing very well here:)
