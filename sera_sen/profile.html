About me!

Hi, my name is Sera (or actually, it's Sara, but way too many people are called like that in my city, so here we are) and I am 26 years old. My favourite books are Throne of Glass, the Silmarillion and Confession of a Mask, which means I love high fantasy, a mixture of Japanese/French/Russian literature and probably crying when I read.
