About me

My name is Nicoletta Pisanu. I was born on 19 April 1988 in a small town in northern Italy, not far from Milano. I currently live in the Netherlands with my husband and our son. I am a student at Leiden University, MA Book and Digital Media Studies.

What I do

I am a professional journalist, employed by an Italian online scientific magazine that covers every aspect of digitalisation: social, economic, political and technological. My work mainly consists of coordinating external contributors, editing articles and writing insights, particularly on the relationship between digital technology and civil rights, crime and other social phenomena. In addition to my main job, I also work as a freelance court reporter for an Italian national newspaper.

Why I am interested in the Media Studies field

I have been working in Journalism for almost twenty years and my professional experience gave me the opportunity to see how this field has evolved thanks to the digital transformation. Today, the media industry faces many challenges, both ethical and social, like the use of AI gen, the spread of social networks and the risks of fake news, disinformation and misinformation.
